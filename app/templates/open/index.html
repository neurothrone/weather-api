Endpoints

  • Get current weather by city name
    GET  /api/1.0/weather/city/?city={city}
    GET  /api/1.0/weather/city/?city=bristol&state=tn&country=us&units=imperial

  • Parameters
    • Required: city={city} - the city you want to get the weather at
    • Optional: state={state} - the state of the city (only works for the US, two-letter state code)
    • Optional: country={country} - country (two-letter country code, defaults to US)
    • Optional: units={units} - units of measurement (metric=Celsius, imperial=Fahrenheit, standard=Kelvin, defaults to metric)
  • Get current weather by coordinates
    GET  /api/1.0/weather/coords/?lat={lat}&lon={lon}

  • Parameters
    • Required: lat={lat} - the latitude coordinate
    • Required: lon={lon} - the longitude coordinate
    • Optional: units={units} - units of measurement (metric: Celsius, imperial: Fahrenheit, standard: Kelvin, defaults to metric)
